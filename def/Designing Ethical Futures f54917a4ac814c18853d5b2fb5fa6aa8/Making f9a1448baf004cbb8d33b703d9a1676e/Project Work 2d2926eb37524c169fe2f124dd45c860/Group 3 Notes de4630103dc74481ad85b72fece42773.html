Do you care about sustainability? And security?

  • If yes,

    then consider... if the security of an element of your device is compromised, then how many more elements of your system would be compromised?

    Sustainability is linked to security in that if you invest in the security of your system, you will have a lower likelihood of needing to recall the hardware. This may mean that you will have to make some hard choices about "green" materials if those "green" materials are less secure.

    For example: What you save in battery life and complexity comes at the price of easy discoverability and exploitability. Whilst BLE does have support for security, it is rarely implemented. When it is implemented it’s often done poorly. The app for this device potentially collects a lot of personal and intimate data -- the app accesses your phone or tablet's microphone, camera, and location tracking. That means a lot of potentially intimate information could be hacked or shared without consent if something went wrong.
