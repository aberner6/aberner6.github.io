This is a empty shell ready for input - it can record people's thoughts in relation to a question (of an event, exhibition, etc.) and so gather their feedback. Also, it allows people to specifically build upon one another's thoughts because you can add your thought to a specific node in a network of thoughts recorded.
The "owners" of this site should put the guiding question in first.
Then, people can record their thoughts and directly comment on one another's thoughts by adding their thought to another's node / make a branch or tangent.
Start by inputting the question you would like your participants to answer.

HOVER over a node to listen to a thought

If you have a comment, add it out loud: put the red circle over that node and HOLD the mouse down while you record your comment.

If you have a new story, CLICK AND HOLD anywhere else in the page to record your new story.
